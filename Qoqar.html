Why we started QoQer
After experiencing the myriads of challenges with sourcing from Asia and the lack of capital disconnect with a lot of aspiring product entrepreneurs, we realized that there was no platform that addressed these two realities. Our team set out to build this platform to make it easy for everyone with a product dream to launch and also get capital where required to start out. We have experienced manufacturing, procurement, design, accounting, shipping and business management experts hailing from all across the globe equipped with the tools that you can leverage on to prepare you for a truly global market place where low pricing and superior quality is key to competitiveness. We work with product designers in America, Canada and Europe and we manufacture in Asia, bringing you the best of both worlds. Qoqer is built for entrepreneurs by entrepreneurs.
